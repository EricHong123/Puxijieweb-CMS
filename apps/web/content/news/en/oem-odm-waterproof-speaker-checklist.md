This checklist helps B2B buyers align requirements with an **OEM/ODM waterproof speaker manufacturer** before sampling.

## 1) Product & performance targets

- Target IPX level (IPX6/IPX7) based on real usage
- Battery capacity and playtime target
- Output power, drivers, and tuning preference

## 2) Compliance & documentation

- Destination market (EU/US/UK/etc.)
- Compliance needs: CE / FCC / RoHS
- Required reports: waterproof test, battery safety, drop test (if needed)

## 3) Branding & packaging

- Private label logo placement (speaker + packaging)
- Box style, inserts, user manual language
- Barcode / labeling requirements

## 4) Commercial terms

- MOQ per model
- Lead time for samples + mass production
- Warranty expectations and after-sales workflow

If you share your destination market and target quantity, you can usually shorten the quote cycle significantly.
